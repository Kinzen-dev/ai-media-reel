The techniqueEach image is luminance-separated into two ink channels, each rasterized as halftone dots on its own angle, then putImageData composited onto warm stock.
MisregistrationA random per-load offset between the plates, nudged a few px by scroll. Reduced-motion freezes the drift and shows the final print.
PerformanceSource capped at 400px, halftone runs once off the rAF loop, results cached as dataURL. Videos decode one at a time, in focus only.
