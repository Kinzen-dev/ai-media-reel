@starting-style burst

Every card detonates into place.

A work gallery where the entire entrance animation lives in CSS. Cards mount staggered and the browser interpolates from a start state declared only inside @starting-style - scale 0.6, blur 8px, an 8deg tilt - to settled. No JS keyframes. Filter a tag and the cards re-insert, firing the burst again.
