    02
    Front Matter

    The Studio, Stated

    The positioning above is the whole thesis. What follows qualifies it: what operate means, how the studio is built, and what cannot fail is meant to say.

    On operating in production
    We hold the pager. Deployment, monitoring, and on-call sit inside our scope, not on the far side of a handoff.
    Studio structure
    Founder-led and deliberately small. Senior hands on every line; no layer of juniors learning on a client budget.
    What cannot-fail means
    Production-critical systems are those whose downtime is counted in lost revenue or lost trust, not inconvenience.

    Doctrine . Four Principles

    Approach

    Architecture first
    We design the system before we write the code.
    Production-worthy
    Demos impress; production endures. We build for the second one.
    Scope discipline
    The most valuable thing we tell clients is what not to build.
    Senior hands
    No juniors learning on your budget. Founder-led, senior hands on every line.

    Doctrine
