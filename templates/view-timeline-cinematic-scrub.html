One timeline. Four beats. Zero scripts.

This entire hero is driven by a single view-timeline on a tall element. Each beat is an ordinary @keyframes bound to that timeline and scoped to a slice of scroll with animation-range, so the whole shot scrubs to scroll position with no JavaScript, no IntersectionObserver and no requestAnimationFrame.

The grade is a real channel blend - an @property custom property animates from cold blue to hot orange and feeds a color-mix(in oklab, ...) accent plus a live filter on the plate. Where the browser cannot drive scroll timelines, an @supports gate renders a static composed final frame instead, and reduced-motion collapses every sweep to its resolved state.
